Press Kit

Brianna Shrum

Is the author of a whole bunch of novels for young adults, including The Liar’s Guide to the Night Sky, Kissing Ezra Holtz (And Other things I Did For Science), The Art of French Kissing, How to Make Out, and Never Never.

Her work has been translated into multiple languages across two continents. She is queer and Jewish and rebelliously lives in the Bible Belt (South Carolina) with her favorite people. She loves all things villainous, gay, magical, and strange, and when she’s not writing, you can usually find her climbing rocks or reading with a chai in her covered-in-literary-quotes-on-the-wall magical writing space.

Brianna is represented by Steven Salpeter at Curtis Brown, Ltd.
